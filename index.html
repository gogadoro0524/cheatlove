FAQ

Frequently asked
questions.

How does the dating profile search work?+
We index public-facing data from major dating apps and cross-reference photos and bios using vector similarity. No private credentials are needed — only what the apps already show to other users in your area.
Is the target ever notified?+
Never. Searches are read-only from our side — no likes, swipes, or messages are sent to anyone. They will never know.
How accurate are the results?+
99% accuracy for accounts using real photos and a known city. For aliased or photo-less profiles, accuracy drops to roughly 70%. We refund in full when no match is found.
How long does a scan take?+
Most searches return in 2–3 minutes. Cross-platform deep searches can take up to 8 minutes.
Is this legal?+
CheatLove only surfaces information already publicly visible to anyone who uses the dating app. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and US data protection laws.
What if no profile is found?+
Full refund within 24 hours — no questions asked, no forms to fill.
Do I need their phone number or email?+
No. First name, approximate age, and a city are usually enough. Uploading a photo boosts accuracy from ~88% to ~99%.
How fresh is the data?+
Profiles are re-indexed every 12–24 hours. Last-seen timestamps are typically accurate within a 30-minute window.
How much does it cost?+
Plans start at $14.99 for a one-time search. See Pricing for the full breakdown.
What happens to my data afterwards?+
Reports auto-purge 30 days after delivery. We never sell or share your data with third parties.
